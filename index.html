Right to Resurrection

If the player has enough *Dark Gems*, they can exchange it for the right to respawn. This right grants him one-time immortality. When he is resurrected, he can continue the game from where he left off.

Diplomacy

If the player has enough *Dark Gems*, He gifted them to Heimdall. Heimdall gives him different abilities in return.

Skill Unlock

The player can use the *Dark Gems* to unlock new skills.

About The Game

The Nine Realms is a game that takes Celtic mythology in a different reality and requires skill and tactics. When you die, you lose all your progress. However, the in-game content you earned before you die remains with you. You can use all these points and abilities for your next journey. You have the right to unlimited resurrection. Each time you die, your character develops more. If you are strong enough and progress enough in the dungeons, a god-boss will appear in front of you. By defeating him/her , you gain new abilities and powers and gain the right to pass to another realm.
